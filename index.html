Welcome to the Annual Conference of the North American Fuzzy Information processing Society NAFIPS 2023

2023 North American Fuzzy Information Processing Society Conference will be held from

Wednesday, May 31 - Friday, June 2, 2023

Digital Futures, University of Cincinnati, Cincinnati, OH, USA.

Announcements

The organizers are continuously monitoring Covid-19 related travel restrictions. We wish to proceed cautiously and within the parameters of public health guidelines to ensure health and safety of all the participants. NAFIPS 2023 will take place in-person and may move on to hybrid mode depending on a surge in Covid-19 cases.

Aim and Scope

As a truly unique and international conference in the areas of fuzzy sets and soft computing, NAFIPS 2023 meeting will bring together scientists, engineers, students, and practitioners working in fuzzy logic and related areas to present their recent research accomplishments. The meeting offers a unique platform for the exchange of ideas, fostering interaction and building multidisciplinary research linkages.

Topics of Interest

  • Mathematical foundations of fuzzy sets and fuzzy systems
  • Approximate reasoning, fuzzy inference models, and soft computing
  • Fuzzy sets and fuzzy logic
  • Fuzzy decision analysis, decision making, optimization, and design
  • Fuzzy system architectures and hardware
  • Fuzzy methods in data analysis, statistics and imprecise probability
  • Fuzzy databases and information retrieval
  • Fuzzy pattern recognition and image processing
  • Fuzzy control and robotics
  • Possibility theory
  • Fuzzy sets and logic in ontology, web, and social networks
  • Fuzzy preference modeling
  • Fuzzy sets in operations research and manufacturing
  • Fuzzy database mining and financial forecasting
  • Fuzzy neural networks
  • Fuzzy systems in machine learning
  • Fuzzy systems in video games
  • Fuzzy systems in bio-medical applications
  • Evolutionary and hybrid systems
  • Explainable AI, trustworthy AI and responsible AI
  • Human-AI interactions and collaboration
  • Intelligent agents and ambient intelligence
  • Learning, adaptive, and evolvable fuzzy systems
